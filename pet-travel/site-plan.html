Pet-Friendly Travel Planner Site Plan

Jose Castro

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help pet owners plan stress-free trips by providing a curated directory of hotels, parks, and restaurants that are verified as pet-friendly. The target audience includes travelers who consider their pets part of the family and need reliable information on amenities like leash-free zones, water stations, and pet menus.

Audience

The target audience consists of pet owners (primarily dog and cat owners) who travel frequently and seek locations that offer more than just "pets allowed" policies. They value high-quality amenities and community-verified ratings.

Dynamic Elements

I will use JavaScript to implement the following features:

  • Data Organization: A complex Array of Objects containing location details like name, type, and specific amenities.
  • Filtering: Use of the .filter() method to allow users to search by category (Hotel, Park, Cafe) or keyword.
  • Sorting: Use of the .sort() method to organize results by highest rating or alphabetically.
  • DOM Interaction: Dynamic injection of HTML cards into the main container using .forEach() and event listeners on the search bar.
